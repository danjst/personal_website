Hello there!

My name is Daniel and I am a biomedical data scientist / clinical informatician born and raised in Manila, Philippines but currently based in Boston, MA. I completed my BS in Molecular, Cell, and Developmental Biology from UCLA in 2019, and am currently pursuing my Masters in Biomedical Informatics at Harvard Medical School.

My research interests lie in developing software to derive insights from large amounts of multimodal clinical and genomic data using machine learning and deep learning architectures. My aim is not only in improving predictive accuracy for the onset of diseases, but also in uncovering the biology of the diseases themselves in order to drive new treatment and therapeutics.

In my spare time I enjoy working out, reading, teaching myself new things (e.g. web dev), curating song playlists, discovering new food, and travelling to different beaches!
